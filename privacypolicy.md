# Privacy Policy for Offline Tides

**Effective Date:** March 3, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Offline Tides. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use our application.

## 2. Information We Collect
Offline Tides is designed to work entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers or any external services.

### Location Data
To provide accurate tide calculations for your area, the app may request access to your device's location. This location data is processed locally on your device to generate tide forecasts and is never shared with us, stored remotely, or transmitted to any third parties.

## 3. How We Use Information
Any data processed by the app, such as your location, custom coordinates, or saved settings, is used solely for the purpose of calculating and displaying tide information and customizing your experience within the app on your device.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services in Offline Tides. Your data remains strictly on your device.

## 5. Children's Privacy
Our application does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13, as we do not collect any personal information at all.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version of this document or within the app.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please feel free to reach out.
